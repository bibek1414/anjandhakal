About

A lifelong learner with a foundation in science and a degree in Business Studies from Tribhuvan University, I believe that education, empathy, and experience are the cornerstones of meaningful progress. Passionate about books and driven by a purpose greater than personal success, my work is guided by a simple yet powerful mission: to make the world a better place where all beings can live in happiness and harmony. Throughout my journey, I've embraced opportunities to help those in need — not for recognition, but because it feels right. My path has never been about collecting titles, but about collecting stories — real moments, deep connections, and personal growth. As I often remind myself: "I would rather collect stories than degrees in my life, for it is the experiences that shape us, not the titles we hold." This belief continues to shape my values, my decisions, and the way I contribute to the world around me.
