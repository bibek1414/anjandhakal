About Me

With a foundation in science and a degree in Business Studies from Tribhuvan University, I've cultivated a unique perspective that bridges analytical thinking with practical business acumen. My journey has been guided by a deep commitment to creating positive change in society.

I believe that education, empathy, and experience are the cornerstones of meaningful progress. My work is driven by a mission to foster environments where all beings can thrive in happiness and harmony.

"I would rather collect stories than degrees in my life, for it is the experiences that shape us, not the titles we hold."

This philosophy has led me to embrace opportunities to help those in need, not for recognition, but because it aligns with my core values. Each experience has contributed to my growth as a compassionate leader and thoughtful contributor to my community.
